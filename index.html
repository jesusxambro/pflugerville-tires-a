SERVING THE COMMUNITY WITH QUALITY AND CARE SINCE 1999!

Established in 1999, Pflugerville Tires began as a dedicated tire repair and replacement service in our local community. Over the years, we have expanded our reach and now proudly serve customers throughout the region, including neighboring counties and communities! Whether you need us at one of our conveniently located facilities or prefer the comfort of your own home or office, we've got you covered. Our fleet of fully equipped mobile tire service units is ready to assist you wherever you are.

At Pflugerville Tires, we specialize in a wide range of tire services. This includes tire repairs, tire replacements, wheel balancing, and alignment for all types of vehicles - from family cars and SUVs to trucks and commercial vehicles, both foreign and domestic. Our commitment to quality service ensures your vehicle is always in safe and reliable hands.

In addition to our core tire services, many of our facilities now offer additional automotive care solutions. These include custom wheel fitting, performance tire installations, and advanced tire pressure monitoring systems. Looking to enhance your vehicle's performance or aesthetic? Our experts are on hand to provide you with the best advice and services in the industry.

Call Pflugerville Tires today to learn more about our services or to schedule an appointment. Experience the difference with quality service that has been trusted since 1999!
